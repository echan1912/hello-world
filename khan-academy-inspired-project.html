Basic info

Biochemistry, as the name suggests is the field of chemistry in biology, aka the molecules of life. Usually this encompasses the study of proteins and nucleic acids, such as DNA. Researchers may refer to this as molecular biology. The more biology-focussed parts of biochemistry, namely cell(ular) biology is also important, as the understand of human organelles and bacteria are useful for the study of molecules involved in processes such as metabolism, replication, protein synthesis, cell-cell interactions and navigating surroundings.Biochemistry is closely related to biotech that involves innovating treatments for disease, ways to enhance research by utilising different biological materials and drug development. Newer hot topics include bioinformatics, computational biology, synthetic biology, metabolic engineering.
